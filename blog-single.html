New Album Release
This album marks a significant exploration of dark and gothic themes, with some tracks reflecting on Halloween. The deluxe edition includes extra bonus tracks and remixes, offering a more immersive experience for fans. It combines the band's signature sound with modern electronic and atmospheric elements
Alice in Chains guitarist Jerry Cantrell returns with this solo album, blending grunge, heavy metal, and blues-rock. Featuring contributions from notable musicians like Duff McKagan (Guns N' Roses) and Robert Trujillo (Metallica), I Want Blood showcases Cantrell's gritty guitar riffs and introspective lyrics. The album deals with darker themes of personal struggle and redemption​
The deluxe edition expands on the original album by offering additional remixes and bonus tracks that provide a deeper dive into these spooky, gothic themes.
